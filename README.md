# Banking-Analytics-Power-BI-Dashboard

Developed an **interactive Banking Analytics Dashboard in Power BI** to analyze customer demographics, account performance, loan portfolios, and financial transactions. The dashboard transforms raw banking data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, KPIs, and DAX calculations, enabling stakeholders to monitor operational performance and make data-driven decisions.

The project includes comprehensive data preparation using Power Query, creation of DAX calculated columns and measures, and implementation of dynamic filters and drill-through functionality to provide an intuitive analytical experience.

**Key Features**
* Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query.
* Developed DAX calculated columns for customer segmentation, age groups, income categories, and credit rating.
* Created business measures such as Total Customers, Total Deposits, Total Loan Amount, Average Credit Score, and Customer Satisfaction.
* Designed an interactive dashboard with KPI cards, maps, pie charts, treemaps, scatter plots, and comparative visualizations.
* Implemented dynamic slicers and cross-filtering for real-time analysis across branches, states, loan types, and account types.
* Analyzed customer behavior, loan performance, branch-wise financial performance, and digital banking adoption.
* Applied professional dashboard design principles to improve readability and decision-making.

**Dashboard Insights**
Branch-wise account balance and customer distribution
Loan portfolio and loan status analysis
Customer segmentation by age, income, occupation, and account type
Credit score and customer satisfaction analysis
State-wise banking performance
Digital banking adoption and customer engagement
